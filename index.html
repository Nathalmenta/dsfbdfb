Our Firm

Design Integrity & Engineering Precision

Operating under the name Nathalia Pimenta Arquitetura e Design, we are a multidisciplinary firm based in Brasilia, Distrito Federal. We do not believe in separating the beauty of architecture from the rigor of engineering.

Led by Nathalia De Sousa Da Silva Pimenta, our firm handles the full spectrum of nonresidential construction. We merge aesthetic consulting with heavy construction capabilities, ensuring that what is designed is buildable, sustainable, and beautiful.

Our scope extends beyond the handover; we offer "Manutencao" (Maintenance) services to ensure your asset retains its value and functionality for years to come.
